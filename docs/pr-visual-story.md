## 🌟 The Evolution Story

### Chapter 1: Multi-Biome Environments with Realistic Pressure

The enhanced system now features three distinct biomes, each exerting unique evolutionary pressures on creature populations:

#### **🌲 Forest Biome: Resource Abundance**
![Forest Biome](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e6fb4d86-1e36-4d07-89ad-ff4a31b24c96)

- **Environment**: "Abundant resources with complex terrain"
- **Selection Pressure**: Favors moderate size, high energy efficiency, intelligence
- **Visual Features**: Green terrain with complex obstacle layout
- **Evolutionary Advantage**: Explorer-Cooperator archetypes dominate (Fitness: 0.881)

#### **🏜️ Desert Biome: Survival Challenge** 
![Desert Biome](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6f696e29-b4b6-4f2c-9dd7-4efa5b2b0a13)

- **Environment**: "Harsh environment with limited resources"
- **Selection Pressure**: Heavily favors small size, high efficiency, speed
- **Visual Features**: Golden/orange terrain with sparse obstacles
- **Evolutionary Challenge**: Higher energy drain (×1.4), reduced reproduction (×0.7)
- **Fitness Impact**: Average drops to 0.763, demonstrating harsh selection pressure

#### **🌊 Ocean Biome: Competitive Waters**
- **Environment**: "Fluid environment with unique challenges" 
- **Selection Pressure**: Favors larger creatures, aggression, exploration
- **Evolutionary Dynamics**: Territorial-Carnivores excel (Behaviors: solitary|explorer|reckless)
- **Fitness Result**: Most challenging environment (0.681 average fitness)
