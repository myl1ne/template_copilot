# Evo v0.3.0 Release Notes

**Release Date**: 2025-10-26  
**Theme**: Advanced Ecosystem & Social Behaviors

## 🎉 Major Features

### 1. Enhanced Multi-Directional Vision System
- **Multi-angle food detection**: Creatures now see in three directions
  - Center vision (forward)
  - Left peripheral (30° left)
  - Right peripheral (30° right)
- **Neural network expanded**: From 16 to 22 inputs
- **Better foraging**: Creatures can detect food clusters and optimize their paths
- **Implementation**: New `getAllFoodPositions()` method and peripheral vision calculations

### 2. Pack Behavior System
- **Cooperative herbivores**: Low-aggression creatures form packs
- **Food sharing mechanics**: Pack members share 30% of food with nearby allies
- **Pack formation**: Herbivores within 8 units automatically form/join packs
- **Pack leadership**: Dynamic leader assignment
- **Social fitness bonus**: Cooperation rewarded in fitness calculations
- **Visual indicators**: Track pack membership in creature inspection

### 3. Predator-Prey Ecosystem
- **Diet types**: Creatures classified as herbivores or carnivores based on aggression gene
  - Herbivores (aggression < 0.4): Green coloring, eat plants
  - Carnivores (aggression > 0.6): Red coloring, hunt prey
- **Hunting mechanics**: Carnivores can attack and eat herbivores
- **Energy gains**: 
  - Herbivores get 20 energy from food pellets
  - Carnivores get 40 energy from successful kills
- **Visual feedback**: Color-coded creatures for easy ecosystem observation
- **Balanced ecosystem**: Predator-prey dynamics create natural population regulation

### 4. Environmental Events System
Inspired by From Dust, dynamic events challenge the ecosystem:

**Event Types**:
- **Abundance** (Yellow indicator): 20 extra food sources spawn temporarily
- **Scarcity** (Orange indicator): 50% of food disappears for 10 seconds
- **Drought** (Red indicator): Environmental stress marker
- **Migration** (Cyan indicator): Food slowly relocates to new area

**Event Mechanics**:
- Events occur every 30 seconds with 70% probability
- Each event lasts 10 seconds
- Visual sky indicators show active event type
- Creatures must adapt strategies to survive

### 5. Enhanced User Interface
New statistics displayed:
- 🌿 Herbivores count
- 🦁 Carnivores count  
- 👥 Active packs count
- 🌍 Current environmental event

## 🔧 Technical Improvements

### Neural Network Enhancements
- **Input expansion**: 16 → 22 sensory inputs
  - Added: left food direction (3)
  - Added: right food direction (3)
  - Total: velocity(3), energy(1), hydration(1), food center(3), food left(3), food right(3), water(3), creature(3), nearby count(1), can mate(1)

### Creature Behavior Improvements
- Diet-based behavior differentiation
- Pack membership tracking
- Food sharing algorithms
- Enhanced attack mechanics with predation bonuses
- Improved fitness calculations for social behaviors

### Environmental System
- New `EnvironmentalEventManager` class
- Four event types with visual and mechanical effects
- Configurable event frequency and duration
- Integration with food and terrain systems

## 📊 Balance Changes

### Energy & Fitness
- Predation energy gain: 20 → 40 (for carnivores)
- Predation fitness reward: 50 → 100
- Attack energy cost: 50% → 30% (for predators)
- Food sharing: 30% of food to nearby pack members

### Population Dynamics
- Herbivores benefit from grouping (pack fitness bonus)
- Carnivores penalized for crowding (solo hunters)
- Natural predator-prey balance emerges

## 🐛 Bug Fixes
- Fixed vision calculations to handle stationary creatures
- Improved pack membership tracking to prevent memory leaks
- Enhanced neural network input normalization

## 🎨 Visual Changes
- Color-coded diet types (green herbivores, red carnivores)
- Environmental event sky indicators
- Pack membership indicators (future enhancement)
- Enhanced UI statistics panel

## 🔬 Emergent Behaviors Observed

From initial testing, the following behaviors have emerged:

1. **Pack Foraging**: Herbivores naturally cluster near food-rich areas
2. **Hunting Patterns**: Carnivores patrol between herbivore groups
3. **Event Adaptation**: Creatures spread out during scarcity, cluster during abundance
4. **Migration Following**: Creatures track food during migration events
5. **Predator Avoidance**: Herbivores show emergent avoidance behaviors (needs more evolution)

## 📈 Performance Notes
- Slight performance impact from 22-input neural networks (acceptable for 20-30 creatures)
- Environmental events have minimal performance overhead
- Pack behavior calculations O(n²) but optimized for <50 creatures

## 🔄 Migration Guide

No breaking changes from v0.2.0. Existing genomes will work but won't have optimized neural weights for new inputs (will initialize randomly).
